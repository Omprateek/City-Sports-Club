Membership Perks

  1. We have three memberships available.
    • 1 month membership
    • 6 Month membership
    • 12 Month membership
  2. Members will be getting a welcome kit from our club on their first membership.
  3. Members can access various sports (indoor as well as outdoor) free for once.
  4. After accesing sports free for once , to access them again they will have to pay a discounted price of the normal charges.(Not applicable for 1 month membership.)

1 Month membership

  • Monthly membership will costs Rs 999.
  • Members will get 30% off on every sports
  • Members can access gym for 50% off.
  • Members will get 20% off on every rentable products.

6 Months membership

  • This membership will costs Rs 4,999.
  • Members will get 50% off on every sports.
  • Members can access gym for free.
  • Members will get 40% off on every rentable products.

12 Months membership

  • Yearly membership will costs Rs 7,999.
  • Members will get 60% off on every sports.
  • Members can access gym for free.
  • Members will get 60% off on every rentable products.
